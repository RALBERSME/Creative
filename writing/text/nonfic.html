Writing non fiction

Writing non fiction is writing about reality. About yourself, the world, nature, animals, planets, science, social situation, relationships, experiences, and much more.

It`s value is based on the fact that it`s content is true. This is how other people learn to understand topics, connections and sometimes even more about themselves.
